About Guitar Chord Mastery

Hi, and welcome to Guitar Chord Mastery! A platform developed for guitar players, by guitar players. I developed this platform by hand to address problems practicing, learning, and reviewing chords that I faced during my journey of teaching myself the guitar. Too often, I find an overwhelming amount of information on simple chord shapes. Currently, this platform is aimed for beginner to intermediate guitarists, as the chord selection involves easy to digest, movable shapes for each chord type and string.

For example, players looking to learn all the C major chords can use comprehensive movable chord shapes based on the root string they are using. The same can be said for a C major 7th chord, and many more. This is a simple approach to fill your chord vocabulary quickly, and get you practicing more often by lowering intimidation.
